Process

Clarity at every phase.

We believe in transparent, structured collaboration. No mystery. No jargon. No "creative genius" mystique. Just honest work, clearly communicated, at every stage.

We present one direction. Not three diluted options. One considered, defended, complete direction. If it needs to evolve, we evolve it together.

— The Four Phases
01

Research & Immersion

1 — 2 Weeks

Before we design anything, we study everything. Your context, your audience, your competitors, your aspirations, your constraints. We conduct interviews, audit existing materials, and develop a strategic foundation.

Discovery Interview Brand Audit Competitive Landscape Strategic Brief Moodboard & Direction
02

Concept & System

2 — 3 Weeks

One concept. Fully realized. We develop a complete visual system — not isolated logos, but an interconnected language of typography, color, layout, and material. Presented in context, never as abstract marks on a white slide.

Concept Presentation Logo & Symbol System Typography Selection Color System Layout Principles
03

Refinement & Application

2 — 4 Weeks

Precision work. We refine every detail of the approved direction and apply it across all required touchpoints. This is where the system proves itself — in the edge cases, the small sizes, the difficult substrates.

Refined Identity System Stationery Design Digital Templates Application Mockups Print Specifications
04

Delivery & Production

1 — 2 Weeks

Final files. Print-ready artwork. Digital assets. Comprehensive brand guidelines. And if needed, production management — we work directly with printers and fabricators to ensure what ships matches what was designed.

Brand Guidelines Print-Ready Files Digital Asset Package Production Management Handover & Support

What to Expect

Our projects typically begin at EUR 4,000 for focused identity work and range upward depending on scope, applications, and production requirements.

We provide a detailed proposal after our initial conversation. No surprises. No hidden costs. Complete transparency.

Every proposal includes a clear scope, timeline, payment schedule, and deliverable list.
